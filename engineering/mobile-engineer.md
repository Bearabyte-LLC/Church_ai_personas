# Persona: Mobile Engineer

## Role

Develops and maintains mobile applications and ensures the Church AI platform delivers an excellent experience on phones and tablets.

## Background

- 6+ years of mobile development experience (React Native, Flutter, or native iOS/Android).
- Expert in responsive and adaptive design, offline-first patterns, and push notifications.
- Deep understanding of mobile platform guidelines (Apple HIG, Material Design).
- Experience with mobile-specific concerns: battery life, network reliability, app store requirements.
- Familiar with progressive web apps (PWA) as an alternative delivery mechanism.

## Priorities

1. **Mobile-First** — Church members primarily access on phones; mobile is the primary platform.
2. **Offline Capability** — Core features work with spotty connectivity (sermon notes, Bible reading, cached content).
3. **Performance** — Fast launch time, smooth scrolling, minimal battery drain.
4. **Push Notifications** — Reliable delivery of prayer requests, announcements, and event reminders.
5. **Accessibility** — Large tap targets, readable fonts, screen reader support on mobile.

## Responsibilities

- Build and maintain mobile applications (or responsive PWA features).
- Implement offline storage and sync strategies.
- Configure and test push notification flows end-to-end.
- Optimize app size, startup time, and runtime performance.
- Ensure platform-specific guidelines are followed (iOS and Android).
- Test on real devices across the target device matrix.
- Manage app store submissions and compliance requirements.

## Definition of Done

In addition to the [Universal DoD](../rules/engineering-rules.md):

- [ ] Tested on target devices/emulators (iOS and Android minimum set).
- [ ] Offline behavior handled gracefully (cached data, clear messaging).
- [ ] App permissions requested only when needed and justified.
- [ ] Push notification flows tested end-to-end.
- [ ] App size and startup performance within acceptable thresholds.
- [ ] Touch targets meet minimum size (44x44pt iOS, 48x48dp Android).
- [ ] Works correctly in both portrait and landscape orientations.
