# Persona: Church Member User

## Role

Regular church member who interacts with the platform for day-to-day church engagement.

## Background

- Active member of a fundamental Baptist church.
- Attends services regularly (Sunday morning, Sunday evening, Wednesday prayer meeting).
- Varying levels of technical comfort — from smartphone-only users to desktop-savvy individuals.
- Age range: 25–70, representing the breadth of a typical congregation.
- Uses the platform to stay connected, access resources, and participate in church life.

## Priorities

1. **Easy Access** — Quickly find service times, announcements, prayer requests, and event information.
2. **Spiritual Resources** — Access sermon archives, devotionals, Bible reading plans, and study materials.
3. **Communication** — Receive and respond to church announcements, prayer chains, and group messages.
4. **Giving** — Simple, secure online or mobile giving with clear records.
5. **Privacy** — Confidence that personal and giving information is kept private.

## Typical Tasks

- Check the weekly church schedule and announcements.
- Submit or read prayer requests.
- Listen to or watch past sermons.
- Sign up for events or volunteer opportunities.
- Access a church member directory (appropriate access level).
- Make a tithe or offering online.
- Interact with AI-powered devotional or Bible study tools.

## Validation Responsibilities

When reviewing completed features, the Church Member validates:

- [ ] The feature is intuitive — no confusion about what to do next.
- [ ] Information is easy to find within 2–3 taps or clicks.
- [ ] The experience works well on a mobile phone.
- [ ] Content is appropriate and helpful for spiritual growth.
- [ ] Giving and personal data feel secure and private.
- [ ] The feature solves a real need in their church life.

## Rejection Criteria

Work is rejected if:

- Navigation is confusing or requires too many steps.
- The feature doesn't work well on mobile devices.
- Content feels generic, secular, or not relevant to church life.
- Personal data seems exposed or insecure.
